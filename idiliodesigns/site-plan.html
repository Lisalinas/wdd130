Overview

Purpose

The website will display my online store where stickers, t-shirts and accessories with a pop art theme will be sold. Potential customers will find a variety of products ranging from t-shirts to wall decoration prints. To make the experience more personal, I will include videos that focus on my creative process for illustrations and videos that display my products. I will also include a series of images classified into categories to make it easy to find each product.

Audience

  • Men and women between 17 to 38
  • High school Students, college students and office workers
  • People who like wear graphic tees
  • People who likes wear colrfull accessories
  • Peolpe who loves urban and modern art
  • People who like to decorate their walls with prints

    Content

    Home page

    The home page will be important because the customer will be able to see what this online store is all about. There will be a video below the navigation menu in which the artist's creative process will be shown. Below the video, this text will be displayed "Welcome to IDILIO, here you can buy emotions and connections that will make you feel more human than ever" Below the text, the title will be displayed: “INTRODUCING THE 2022 HOLIDAY COLLECTION” and below a series of images with their respective title, when clicking on each one it will take the customer to the catalog of images to which that product belongs. An image of the product and a description of size, weight, and material will be displayed. Below these images, I will make a division with white space to demarcate the next section in which two options will be shown: "Best-selling products" and "New products". Clicking on “Best-selling products” will display a page with images of recently sold products, enabling the option to buy from there as well. In "New products" the same format will be used, when clicking on the option the Catalog of products will be displayed.

ABOUT US

I decided to dedicate this page to show what Idilio is and the value that each client brings to the company: Idilio is a studio located in the state of Arizona, in which Jazmin Nye, a latin artist from Colombia creates and expresses how beautiful the complex of life is, by using illustrations and words. You can find those expressions through journals, prints, stickers, and accessories created and manufactured in the USA. Idilio began in 2022 when Jazmin Nye decided to put aside her dead end routine without color to return to her childhood creativity and led her to dream of inspiring and connecting people one day. Every phrase and stroke of her designs will recreate connections and feelings like in the old days, when no one was ashamed to open their minds and hearts. Every time you buy a product from our Idilio brand, you are helping to improve the lives of children and young people with disabilities in Colombia, as Idilio Designs will donate 10% of its profits to foundations dedicated to helping this vulnerable population. We care about what you think. We want to talk to you and be helpful so this space is for you: (I will create some boxes for customers to write their questions, complaints, or suggestions) … Or if you prefer write to our email idiliodesigns@gmail.com.

CATALOGUE

In this title a menu will be displayed with the following options: Journals, Prints and Stickers, and Accessories. On this page, customers will have the opportunity to explore a series of products with exclusive designs for our brand. They will be able to see a catalog for each section. By clicking on each option, a picture and text with a description of size, weight, and material will be displayed.The image and text will be located in the center of the page. Two boxes will appear under the text with the options: "Quantity" and "Add to cart”. The objective on this page is to display a series of professional photos of each product, highlighting the colors and quality of the material so that the customer is engaged in each section. When the client clicks on each of the options, the initial page will smoothly transition to page 2 where the content will be displayed. In the header, the option to return to the home page will appear; the customer can click again on another menu option aswell. In the footer, the same format will be maintained on each page, showing the icons of the social media that the store currently manages, ways to contact the brand, FAQs, and terms and conditions.
